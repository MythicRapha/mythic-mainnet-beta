# DECLARATION OF FORMATION
# MYTHIC FOUNDATION
## A Decentralized Unincorporated Nonprofit Association
### Under Wyoming Statutes §§ 17-36-101 through 17-36-115

---

**BE IT KNOWN** that on this 25th day of February, 2026, the members of Mythic Foundation, a decentralized community of approximately 300 holders of the MYTH digital token, do hereby declare the formation of a **Decentralized Unincorporated Nonprofit Association ("DUNA")** under the laws of the State of Wyoming.

---

## I. FORMATION

Pursuant to Wyoming Statute § 17-36-102, this Association is formed by the mutual consent of its members, numbering in excess of one hundred (100) natural or juridical persons, united for a common nonprofit purpose.

The Association comes into existence upon the adoption of its Governing Principles by its members, effective as of the date written above.

## III. MEMBERS

As of the date of formation, the Association has approximately **300 members**, identified as holders of the MYTH token at the following addresses:

- **Solana L1:** Token mint `5UP2iL9DefXC3yovX9b4XG2EiCnyxuVo3S2F6ik5pump`
- **Mythic L2:** Native MYTH token (gas token of the Mythic L2 network)

Membership is open, voluntary, and transferable through the acquisition or disposal of MYTH tokens.

## IV. NONPROFIT STATUS

This Association is organized and shall be operated exclusively for nonprofit purposes. No part of the net earnings of the Association shall inure to the benefit of any member, administrator, or private individual. All revenue shall be applied exclusively toward the development, maintenance, and governance of the Mythic blockchain network and ecosystem.

## V. LIMITED LIABILITY

Pursuant to Wyoming Statute § 17-36-109, no member or administrator of this Association shall be personally liable for the debts, obligations, or liabilities of the Association solely by reason of being or acting as a member or administrator.

The initial Administrator of the Association is designated to serve until the first governance election conducted through the on-chain Governance Program (MythGov111111111111111111111111111111111111).

## VII. LEGAL STANDING

This Declaration, together with the adopted Governing Principles, establishes Mythic Foundation as a legal entity under Wyoming law with the capacity to:

- Enter into contracts and agreements
- Own, hold, and dispose of real and personal property
- Sue and be sued in its own name
- Maintain bank and digital asset accounts
- Engage in any lawful activity in furtherance of its purposes
